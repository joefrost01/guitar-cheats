## ‚öôÔ∏è Easy Prince Hacks
1. **Percussive Muting** ‚≠ê‚≠ê: Practice damping strings immediately after chord hits
2. **Short Phrase Focus** ‚≠ê‚≠ê: Create brief, memorable melodic statements rather than long solos

5. **Precise Bends** ‚≠ê‚≠ê‚≠ê: Practice hitting exact pitches with bends, especially half-step bends
6. **Dynamic Contrast** ‚≠ê‚≠ê: Alternate between whisper-quiet and aggressive playing
